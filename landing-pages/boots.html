PWI + Saf-Gard Boot Discounts

PWI has partnered with Saf-Gard Shoes to provide you with an option to purchase your work boots at a discounted price and have it payroll deducted! All shoes will automatically be discounted 10-25%.

You'll need to know your employee ID which is simply your full 8-digit birthday minus any slashes or hyphens (for example 01/01/2001 becomes 01012001).

Ways to Shop:

  1. Visit the store in Goshen to see and try on shoes. Let them know that you are a PWI employee.
    • 4542 Elkhart Rd, Suite 700, Goshen Village Shoppes Shopping Center, Elkhart, IN 46517
    • Pay in full at that time or ask them to payroll deduct it.
    • If you choose payroll deduction, you'll take your shoes home and PWI will deduct the total amount split up over your next 4 paychecks.
  2. Use the button below "Start Shopping" and enter your employee id.
    • Shop just like you would in any online store. Prices will automatically be your discounted prices.
    • Make your purchase and your bill will automatically be payroll deducted over your next 4 checks.
    • Shoes will be delivered to your front door.
